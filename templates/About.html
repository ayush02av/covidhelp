ABOUT US

We Ayush Verma and Vaibhav Kumar students of ADGITM, Delhi pursing B.tech (IT) made this website with the objective to help people deal with the unprecedented crisis that this pandemic has brought upon us.


We have designed this website to help people find verified leads for necessary medical supplies. It is a user friendly website which enables people to not only find verified leads but also submit them.


If this website even helps one person in getting the necessary medical supplies then we will consider that our objective behind developing this website is achieved. Stay safe and take care.
