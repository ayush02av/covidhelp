India is witnessing the largest vaccination drive in the world, with the government allowing vaccination for people who are 18 years or older the 3rd phase of vaccination has started. In order to get vaccinated, one has to book a slot at their preferred vaccination centre on the COWIN Platform that the government has provided by using Aadhar card. However, due to the difference between demand and supply booking a vaccination slot can be tricky. In order to curb this issue we have introduced a feature on our website through which a user can find the nearest available vaccination centre by entering their area code. There are certain safeguards kept in mind before getting vaccinated and these are –
1. Social distancing and Mask It is important to ensure social distancing at the vaccination center to reduce the risk of transmission. In order to ensure maximum safety, it is advisable to wear a mask at all times.
2. Consult a Doctor Let the health care professional know if you have any medical conditions that could be considered precautions, such as pregnancy or a compromised immune system.
3. Monitoring The vaccine administrator should observe you for about 30 minutes after the vaccine is administered to make sure you don’t have any immediate reactions.
4. Side Effects One should be prepared for mild side effects after administration of vaccine such as –
a. Arm soreness at the injection site
b. Mild fever
c. Fatigue
d. Headaches
e. Muscle or joint aches
f. Chills However, If any of these symptoms continue for more than a few days or if you experience a more severe reaction, then contact a doctor immediately.
